Vertical Farm :
Growing Food on a Pocket-sized Space

Some of us are exploring the potential for growing edible plants off-soil within an urban area, expanding the growing space upwards.




Donation




This project is run by team of green geeks in their spare time, generously donating some brain juice and muscle power. Donations are welcome!


How to get involved


Everyone is welcome to partake in the building effort or help us gather all items required to make it happen.
If you wish to participate, drop us a line using the form below - Many thanks!
